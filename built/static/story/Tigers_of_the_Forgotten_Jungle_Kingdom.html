Tigers_of_the_Forgotten_Jungle_Kingdom

In the heart of India, where the sun shines bright, Lived Bjorn, a tiger, with a coat of golden light. He roamed the Forgotten Jungle Kingdom, so grand, With its towering trees, and its winding sand.

Bjorn loved to explore, with his curious eyes, He'd chase after butterflies, and watch the clouds rise. He'd play with the monkeys, and laugh with glee, In the jungle's magic, where wonder waited to see.

One day, while wandering, Bjorn stumbled upon a clue, A hidden treasure map, with a riddle or two. He followed the path, through the jungle so dark, And discovered a secret, that left him in a spark.

A hidden waterfall, with a pool so blue, Reflected the stars, and the moon shining through. Bjorn drank from the pool, and felt refreshed and new, And knew that he'd found, a treasure true.

The jungle creatures, all gathered 'round, To celebrate Bjorn's find, with a joyful sound. The monkeys danced, and the birds sang with glee, In the Forgotten Jungle Kingdom, where wonder waited to see.

Bjorn returned home, with a heart full of cheer, And a story to tell, of his jungle adventure so clear. He learned that day, that the jungle's secrets are vast, And that with curiosity, and courage, we can discover at last.
